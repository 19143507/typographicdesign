Meet Our Founder

Hello everyone, I'm Patrick, the founder of Carlligraphy!

My years on planet Earth only add up to a measly 19 years, but if there's one thing you should know about my time so far, it's that it consists mostly of "fan-boying" over these delectable pieces of machinery, more commonly known as cars.

Fast cars are, of course, even better!

I was first introduced to the concept of typography in school and it was a part of my favourite subject: English.

Essentially, this is how Carlligraphy came into existence; a mere question of whether one could combine their favourite pasttime with their favourite academic subject.

That question is what we are trying to answer, so let's get to work and let's see how far we can take it!

Typography & Cars?

From Formula 1 to Formula 4, from Rallycross to Karting; typography influences them all, and in a plethora of different ways too.

Not only does typography influence the upper echelons of motor racing, but it also influences our own personal cars as well.

Your very own car and the whole process of you purchasing it have been manipulated by typography.

Weird right?

I found this especially baffling at first, but one glance into the real world, and you will agree that everything I've said so far is true!
